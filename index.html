Apex MMA is a full-time mma gym located in Mansfield, England. It was founded in 2015 when the mma scene began growing at a rapid rate. Before the current gym became an mma gym it was a commercial weight lifting gym. The intention behind Apex MMA was to create a sanctuary for people of all ages to learn different types of martial arts or the mixed martial arts.

Boxing

Lets go back to the basics and start where every great champion starts. Whether your learning to compete or just for fitness, you will learn how to strike in our boxing classes.

Kickboxing

Build resilience, condition your body and learn to strike with our kickboxing class. This class combines both punches and kicks to provide you with the ultimate workout. You will learn the most basic of kicks to the advanced ones you see Apex champions use.

MMA

Don't have time for all the other combat classes? Just join our MMA class to get the best of all the worlds. In this class you'll learn striking techniques, defensive strategies and how to submit the biggest opponaents.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Sometimes it isn't about being strong or being big. Sometimes it's about technique, in brazilian jiu-jitsu you'll learn exactly that. The technique used by some of the best champions to exist.

Mauy Thai

Take a dive into the Thai culture with our mauy thai class. In this class you'll learn the art of 8 limb combat. Mauy thai combines a variety of punches, kicks, knees and elbows to deal devistating damage to your opponents.

1-to-1 Coaching

Take your health and fitness to a new level by joining one of our 1-to-1 coaching sessions. These sessions allow the coach to decicate their full time to you.
