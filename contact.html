FAQs:

Q: How old do children need to be to start the classes?

A: The minimum age we can start training is age 4

Q: Do you have beginner classes?

A: We don't have classes specifically for beginners. We believe that training everyone together will not only promote development but it will also allow you to learn through watching others.

Q: What should I wear to the classes?

A: Prefferably some comfortable shorts or joggers but it doesn't matter as long as you're able to move easily in them.
